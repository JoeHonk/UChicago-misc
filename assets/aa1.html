Submit an Event

To Create an Event: Submit events you would like to promote via the Online Community’s Event Calendar.

  • Events must be relevant to the University of Chicago alumni community and are subject to approval by a member of the Alumni Association staff.
  • The appropriate staff member will follow up with you within two business days.
  • Approved events will usually appear on the Alumni, Friends & Families website within one week of submission.

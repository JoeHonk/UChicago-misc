Janet D. Rowley Discovery Fund

Pursuing breakthrough cancer research in the name of a legend.

Janet D. Rowley, LAB’42, PhB’45, SB’46, MD’48    
(1925–2013)

Her gift for challenging accepted wisdom led her to discover the genetic basis of cancer, revolutionizing how we think about and treat cancer.

Your gift to the Janet D. Rowley Discovery Fund will allow the Comprehensive Cancer Center’s physician-scientists and researchers to pursue their best ideas. Like Rowley, they have a goal to make the discoveries that can stop cancer.

Everybody has a gift. Imagine the impact yours can make.

CVV Digits Explained

To make a payment with your credit/debit card, we need to know the CVV number from the reverse of the card. This is used by the credit card company to identify you as having the card in your possession when placing the order. Your card will not be authorised and hence your order will be rejected if you do not enter the correct digits.

Depending on the age of the card the CVV number is the 3 or 4 digit number printed on the signature strip on the reverse of the card as shown below.

American Express cards show the card security code (also referred to as the CID number) printed above the imprinted card number on the front of the card (on either the left or right)**.

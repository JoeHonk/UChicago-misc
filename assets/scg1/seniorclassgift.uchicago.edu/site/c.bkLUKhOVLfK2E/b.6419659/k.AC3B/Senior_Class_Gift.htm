WHat will your legacy be?


your senior class gift 

As one of the premier senior class gift campaigns in the country, our campaign is a student-led initiative supporting student life. Students have the option of giving to the College Fund, Dean's Fund for Student Life, the Jeff Metcalf Internship Program, and Odyssey Scholarships. The Senior Class Gift is dedicated to sustaining the University’s commitment to excellence in undergraduate education and student life. Student and alumni giving is what makes a great institution like UChicago great.

what we support

Our gift goes to the College Fund, the Dean's Fund for Student Life, the Jeff Metcalf Internship Program, and the Odyssey Scholarships. These funds are the primary sources of general support for students.

The College Fund ensures that the University can meet its most pressing student life needs that would otherwise go unfunded including financial aid, study abroad, and internships.

The Dean's Fund for Student Life provides funding for student projects and supports student innovation.

The Jeff Metcalf Internship Program offers paid internships exclusively to UChicago undergraduates.

The Odyssey Scholarships gives financial aid to ensure that the most qualified students are able to attend regardless of financial capacity.

Tuition has never covered the full cost of a Chicago education. These funds help make up the difference.

Odyssey Giving Challenge

The new $100 million plan will increase funding for college prep programs in Chicago Public Schools; reduce family contributions for books and travel; and provide resources for students to fully engage in career advancement and mentorships.

Harriet Heyman, AM'72, and Sir Michael Moritz are funding 50% of the new enrichment plan. It is our challenge -- as alumni, parents, families, and friends -- to fund the remaining $50 million within 5 years.

Odyssey Endowing Our Commitment

Odyssey was established in 2007 through a $100 million gift -- then the largest in University history. The anonymous benefactor, dubbed Homer, funded annual costs while the University created an endowment to underwrite the program on a permanent basis.

Odyssey has exceeded endowment goals every year thanks to the generosity of more than 10,000 donors. Odyssey alumni believe in the program; nearly 80% are donors. Odyssey is a manifestation of our community's commitment to merit, self-achievement, and disciplined work.

Other ways to give

Looking for information about how to give in other ways? The University gratefully accepts gifts of securities, retirement plan assets, and real estate, as well as bequests and life income gifts.


Click here to give cash by phone, mail, wire transfer, or donor-advised fund. The University of Chicago's TIN (taxpayer identification number) is 36-2177139.

Safe & Secure

The security of your information is very important to us. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to protect the security of your online gift information.

Support your College Reunion Class Gift

Make a gift in honor of your Reunion by June 30 to help your class meet its goals.

Share your support

When you share your support you magnify the impact of your gift by inspiring others to give. The details of your gift and private information will not be shared.

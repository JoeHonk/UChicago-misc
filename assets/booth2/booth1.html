Join the Campaign

The University of Chicago Campaign: Inquiry & Impact will bolster Chicago Booth’s reputation as a premier business school, paving the way for leaders whose groundbreaking ideas and discoveries will have broad impact around the globe.

It is because of the unwavering support from our donors that Booth remains a world-renowned school committed to creating knowledge with enduring impact and influencing and educating current and future leaders. Today, we invite you to support our faculty and students as they preserve Booth’s tradition of defining future inquiry.

Whether you offer financial support, volunteer, or attend an event, your involvement is critical to the success of our campaign. How will you participate?

Make a gift

Help us make a lasting impact through your gift of any size, which will count toward our $850 million fundraising goal.

Planned giving

Planned gifts offer vital future support for students, faculty, programs, and facilities at Chicago Booth while also providing advantages such as increased tax benefits or an income stream. Learn more about your many gift planning options.

Attend an event

Get involved with the Chicago Booth community. Find an event near you.

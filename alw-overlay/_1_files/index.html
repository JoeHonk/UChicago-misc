What difference can one day make?

Your support is key to the success of UChicago’s students and programs, faculty and researchers, practioners and patients. What if you could watch the seed of that support—your gift—combine with thousands of other gifts over the span of 24 hours to become something that will have a tangible, lasting impact at UChicago and beyond?

Scholarships that open the doors of the College to exceptional and deserving students. Opportunities that inspire faculty and students to deepen their scholarship, innovate, and explore their creative and professional passions. Research ideas that aim to advance treatment for patients in Chicago and beyond. New initiatives that seek to improve schools across the country and the lives of billions around the globe.

That’s what we’ll do together on February 24-25, 2016, during 24Hour Impact. Every gift counts. And special donor challenges make gifts across the University count even more.

What a difference one day can make. Give during 24Hour Impact and watch your gift grow from more to more.

Relish the Challenge

Grow a tradition of impact

Last April, the University of Chicago launched our community’s first giving day, 24Hour Impact, an online celebration of philanthropic impact. More than 2,300 alumni, students, parents, and friends—a record 96 donors per hour—made gifts to support people and programs with over $1.4 million in funding.

This year’s event will be even bigger. Your support will build on the success of the first giving day, strengthening our tradition of community impact. Your gift also counts toward the University of Chicago Campaign: Inquiry and Impact, which aims to raise $4.5 billion and engage 125,000 alumni.
